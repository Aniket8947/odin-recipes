MATAR PANEER

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Sachet MAGGI Masala-ae-Magic
  • 2 Tablespoons Oil
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomato
  • 0.5 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon corriander powder
  • 200 gms peas
  • 300 gms paneer
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons corriander leaves

METHOD

  • Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
  • Heat oil In a pressure cooker, add chopped onions and fry till golden brown. Add ginger garlic paste and fry for 5 minutes. Now add the tomatoes and sauté till soft.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and coriander powder. Fry for another minute. Add peas and sauté for 2-3 minutes. Then, add the paneer cubes, water, MAGGI Masala ae Magic and salt to taste. Mix well.
  • Cover the pressure cooker and cook for 2 minutes after the first whistle.
  • Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
