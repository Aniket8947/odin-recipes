DALFRY

INGREDIENTS

  • Arhar dal/Split Pigeon peas.
  • Salt, red chilli powder, turmeric.
  • chopped ginger, garlic, green chilli, coriander leaves, lime juice.
  • Onions
  • fresh tomato
  • Ghee, cumin seeds, asafoetida, whole red chillies, Kashmiri red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder etc.

METHOD

  • Heat oil in a pan and temper with cumin seeds. Next add the slit green chilies, red chilies, ginger and saute for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add the sliced onions and saute till translucent.
  • Now add the chopped tomatoes and saute till cooked and mushy.
  • Once tomatoes are well cooked add salt,turmeric powder and coriander powder. Mix and cook for 4-5 minutes.
  • Now add the mashed toor dal and mix well. Also add 1 cup dal cooked water or normal water.
  • Simmer the dal until it thickens a bit for around 6-8 minutes. Add garam masala powder and mix well.
  • Add chopped cilantro leaves and kasuri methi.
  • Remove from flame lemon juice mix well and serve dal fry hot.
